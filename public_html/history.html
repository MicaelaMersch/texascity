How the name came to be was when Texas settlers wanted to leave from Texas City, Texas due to the extreme heat and harsh life of Texas City, Texas. They traveled by wagons and horse back, dealing with harsh weather and troubles in 1800s. Once in Iowa they made the area they settled at the new Texas City!
