About Texas City!

According to the 2018 United States Census Bureau, the current population of Texas City is 212,546. It is a larger town with a small town feeling, meaning it is not intimidating nor a massive city. Located in geographic center of Iowa. Texas City is the fastest growing city and fastest growing city for medical fields! We also have one of the best Hospitals in the state of Iowa called Union Texas City Hospital! The weather typically in Texas City is mild to hot summers and harsh winters. Many tourist come to visit Texas City because of its inviting atmosphere.
